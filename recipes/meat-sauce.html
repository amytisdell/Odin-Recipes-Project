Homemade Meat Sauce

Enjoy this meat sauce all by itself over a bowl of steamy pasta, or use it as an ingredient in Grandma's Famous Lasagna!

Ingredents
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • 5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 lb sweet italian sausage, skin removed
  • 1 lb spicy italian sausage, skin removed
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 32 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 1 6 oz can tomato paste
  • 1 8 oz jar tomato sauce
  • 3 tbsp italian seasoning
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 bay leaves
Instructions
  1. heat oil in dutch oven over medium-high heat
  2. brown onions in oil until translucent
  3. add sweet italian sausage and spicy italian sausage to onions, crumbling as it browns
  4. Add ground beef and continue to brown all meats. Add garlic.
  5. Add remaining ingredients and mix thoroughly. Bring to a simmer and lower heat to low.
  6. Allow sauce to simmer for 1-2 hours
  7. remove bay leaves and serve.
